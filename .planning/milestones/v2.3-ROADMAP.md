## Milestone Summary

**Key Decisions:**
- Table not Board for game surface (Board implies physical constraint)
- Seat not Position for players (Position is overloaded with grid position)
- Pick not Selection for choices (Pick conveys intent of choosing)
- Deprecation aliases for renamed APIs (allows gradual migration)

**Issues Resolved:**
- All 2 orphaned `player.position` references fixed (mcts-bot.ts, debug.ts)
- Integration checker verified cross-phase wiring
- All 5 E2E flows verified complete
