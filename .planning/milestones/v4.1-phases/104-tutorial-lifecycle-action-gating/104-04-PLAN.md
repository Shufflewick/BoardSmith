modes never diverge (RESEARCH Pitfall 4 / parity hard-rule).

gate reasons (`disabledActions`) + the active step on the wire. Lifecycle mutates the serialized
`tutorialProgress` (Plan 104-01) so undo rewinds the step for free; both `buildPlayerState` and
`createPlayerView` must populate the SAME slots or the two client projections diverge.

cross-layer integration test (engine progress -> controller -> client projection).
