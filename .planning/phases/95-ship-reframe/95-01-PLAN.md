<objective>
Replace the split-screen scaffold with a single-UI scaffold driven by a new `boardsmith.json "ui"` field. A fresh `boardsmith init` produces a game that opens immediately in the auto-UI, with an optional empty custom-UI stub marked "start here". Implements D-01/D-02/D-03 and SHIP-03; lays the single-static-import foundation that SHIP-02's tree-shaking proof (Plan 03) depends on.

Purpose: The split-screen "Custom UI vs Auto-Generated UI" framing lives entirely in `generateAppVue()`. Removing it and making the import selectable via config.ui makes the auto-UI a shippable production peer and makes ordinary tree-shaking able to drop unselected UIs.
Output: Rewritten generator functions, a new scaffold-generator test file, and an optional `"ui"` validation in validate.ts.
</objective>
